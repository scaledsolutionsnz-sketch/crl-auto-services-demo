About CRL

Belfast's Local
Auto Experts

CRL Auto Services is Cory's workshop in Belfast, Christchurch. We've built a reputation around straightforward service — we tell you what needs doing, what can wait, and we charge fairly for everything in between.

We service all makes and models, from small hatchbacks to utes, vans, and everything in between. Whether you're due for your annual WOF, need new tyres, or something's not right under the bonnet — bring it in.

  • Honest diagnosis — we won't recommend work you don't need
  • Fair, upfront pricing before any work begins
  • Quality parts and workmanship on every job
  • Quick turnaround — we respect your time
